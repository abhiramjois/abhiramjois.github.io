Gombe Habba

Gombe habba is a digital archive of various doll and toy handicrafts in India. It also has a community feature where small scale makers and enthusiats can connect with each other and work towards building acommunity to protect these practices.

YogharPe

YogharPe is a posture accuracy measurement model that aims as being an aid to people who are self learning Yoga at home. It provides audio and visual feedback on the accuracy of the pose performed by the user.

Thatte Thumba Kathegalu

A platter full of stories. A walk curated around the streets of commercial aims at putting those people and places on a map who have no identity in a digital era. It tells unque stories about working housewives, migrants and small businesses etc using food as a medium of narrative

Algori

Algori aims at being a creative tool for education of algorithms. It uses the narrative of a role playing stratergy game and personifies different parts of an algorithm as characters of the game. It aims at viewing algorithms more tangibly.
